Swedavias UtryckningsrapportSystem (SUS)



Swedavia's UtryckningsrapportSystem (SUS) is an emergency management platform at Arlanda Airport. As its UX Designer, I'm spearheading the site's redesign to enhance user experience. I've established the UI/UX system, collaborating closely with a team and clients from inception, ensuring seamless design and function integration.

Vasttrafik To-Go app



This project was completed by a four-member group. We leveraged our UX research findings to modify the design of the “Västtrafik To-Go app” with the goal of resolving the issues identified during the user testing phase. I served as the scrum master and developer for this project. You can find more detailed case studies about this project on my Dribbble page.

Museum Audio-tour App



This was my first UX design project, which involved creating a prototype for an audio-tour mobile application for museums. The primary goal of this app is to offer audio assistance to museum and art gallery visitors, regardless of age or gender.
