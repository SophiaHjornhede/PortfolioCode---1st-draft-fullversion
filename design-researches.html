LumeLink: Bridging Reality and Virtuality



LumeLink is a speculative design product, stemming from discursive design research. Set in the aftermath of the tech singularity, this diminutive sim card integrates with the visual cortex, offering a gateway to virtual reality. Drawing parallels to "Upload (2020)", LumeLink combines ecological mindfulness with the groundbreaking concept of post-mortem consciousness preservation. Dive into a narrative where life, the beyond, and virtual realms intertwine, showcasing future-centric design.

VaPA: Voice Avatar Personal Assistant



Emerging from a research quest in AI and big data design, VaPA poses the question: "In what ways can VaPA's design incorporate adaptive learning to effectively understand and mimic user behavior for personalized communication assistance?" Tailored for mute individuals and multitaskers, VaPA seamlessly blends empathy and efficiency. With user-centric design and a commitment to privacy, this innovative tool is set to redefine digital communication. Dive into a future where tech truly comprehends your needs.
